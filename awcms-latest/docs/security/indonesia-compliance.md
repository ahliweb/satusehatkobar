# Indonesia Compliance Baseline

## Referenced Regulations

- UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 tentang Pelindungan Data Pribadi
- PP No. 71 Tahun 2019 tentang Penyelenggaraan Sistem dan Transaksi Elektronik

## Baseline Expectations

- define privacy notice and consent flows where personal data is collected
- define correction, export, and deletion request workflows
- define retention and destruction expectations for personal data and audit records
- review public-sector or government deployment governance where applicable
- maintain auditability for content and administrative actions

## Practical AWCMS-Micro Mapping

- Privacy notice and consent: handled at the deploying project level, documented before production go-live.
- Export, delete, and correction: should be documented through administrative procedures and application workflows.
- Retention and audit expectations: should be defined for D1 data, R2 uploads, and operational logs.
- Public-sector considerations: should include approval paths, operator accountability, and change-review evidence.
